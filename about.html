About the Afghan Land Restaurant!

The Afghan Land Restaurant was first built in 2023 in San Diego, California. It is the first Afghan restaurant, that serves as the main gate of food from South Asian countries, particularly Afghanistan. We are proud to have special Afghan Dishes and meals. We offer great Palaw, Qabeli, Ashak, Mantoo, Tekka Kebab, Chapli Kabab, Boolani, and Chicken Karai. We offer different types of deserts like cake, Halwa, Jelabi, Jelabi, Sheerpeera, Melon, Ferni, and a wonderful great tea which comes with Safaron and True Cardamom.

Popular Orders

Palaw (Afghan Rice with beef) Ashak (Chives inside dough) Jalabi (Afghan Famous sweet) Tikka Kabab(Cooked Ground Meat)
