About the Afghan Land Resturant!

The Afghan Land Resturant was first built in 2023 in San Diego, California. It is the firtst Afghan resturant, that serves as the main gate of food from South Asian countries, particularly Afghanistan. We are proud to have special Afghan Dishes and meals. We offer great Palaw, Qabeli, Ashak, Mantoo, Tekka Kebab, Chapli Kabab, Boolani, Chicken Karai. We offer differnt types of deserts like cake, Halwa, Jelabi, Jelabi, Sheerpeera, Melon, Ferni, and a wonderful great tea which comes with Safaron and True Cardamom.

Popular Orders

Palaw (Afghan Rice with beef) Ashak (Chives inside dough) Jalabi (Afghan Famous sweet) Tikka Kabab(Cooked Grind Meat)
